Data Quality & Limitations

Strengths
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Data spans multiple years and countries
  • Standardized Metrics: World Bank indicators follow international standards
  • Rich Variables: Wide range of socio-economic and health indicators
  • Public Access: Open data sources with transparent methodologies
Limitations
  • Missing Values: Some countries have incomplete data for certain years
  • Reporting Variations: Different countries may use different reporting methods
  • Time Lag: Latest data may be 1-2 years old due to collection and processing
  • Sample Bias: Survey data may not represent all demographics equally
Note: All data has been cleaned and processed for consistency. Original data sources should be consulted for methodology details and raw data.
